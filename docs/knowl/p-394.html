The Neron model of \(E/K\) is an arithmetic surface \(\mathcal E/K\) whose generic fibre is the given elliptic curvs. It is such that every point of \(E\) gives a point of \(\mathcal E\) and such that the group law on \(E\) extends to make \(\mathcal E\) into a group (as a scheme over \(R\)).
